How to Draw:

1. Start off by selecting a shape
button
2. Selected a colour from the
colour palette
3. Drag on the WHITE canvas
to draw your shape in the colour
you chose
4. If you want to undo a shape
click the 'Undo' button. If you
want to start from the beginning
with a clean canvas click the
'Reset' button
5. Have fun and create your
masterpiece!
